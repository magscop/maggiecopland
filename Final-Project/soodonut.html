Campaign Overview

Challenge

Since 2016, Tim Hortons has joined forces with Special Olympics Canada to provide support for athletes with intellectual disabilities nationwide. During a designated period in February, purchasing a Special Olympics Donut from Tim Hortons directly contributes to these athletes' cause. All proceeds generated from these donut sales are funneled directly into local Special Olympics programs, providing vital assistance to thousands of intellectually disabled athletes across Canada.

For the 2024 Tim Hortons Campaign, I represented Special Olympics Ontario's marketing efforts. As the largest province in terms of athlete numbers and engagement, we were entrusted with boosting awareness and fundraising for the campaign. Motivated by the previous campaign's lack of visibility and engagement, I spearheaded a comprehensive initiative aimed at spotlighting our exceptional athletes and community. Through strategic content development and a more organized advertising approach, our marketing and creative efforts yielded unprecedented results. This led to record-breaking sales figures, heightened online engagement, and an expanded social network, making it the most successful campaign to date.

Execution

General Overview

The 2024 Special Olympics Donut Campaign proved to be an outstanding success surpassing all previous Tim’s campaigns by raising over $880,000. This remarkable achievement can be credited to the strategic planning and heightened awareness brought to the campaign by Special Olympics Ontario (SOO), along with the unwavering support from one of SOO’s greatest assets – our diverse network of communities.

Special Olympics Ontario contributed to 49% of the overall sales in 2024, leading to more than a 50% boost in total provincial sales compared to the previous year. A key factor in the campaign’s triumph was the foresight in developing content well in advance of the launch, addressing past feedback and ensuring a well-prepared audience when the campaign kicked off.

Campaign Strategy

Both the marketing and creative strategy for the 2024 Tim’s campaign embodied a proactive, accessible, and inclusive approach, meticulously crafted to propel engagement and awareness to new heights. Following an analysis of previous campaigns, which highlighted deficiencies such as limited community involvement, low engagement from Special Olympics Ontario, and uninspiring creative devoid of local athletes, we embarked on a strategy prioritizing community empowerment. By providing tools and compelling content, we facilitated seamless sharing and engagement, fostering a vibrant online community.

We devloped a landing page, multiple photo contests, over 35 unique graphics, 4 social media videos and ran 3 different types of ad campaigns using Meta.

Conclusion

The Results

Special Olympics Ontario’s successful campaign prioritized community engagement through an organic approach. Utilizing user-generated content, community stories, and grassroots involvement via communities, the initiative fostered genuine support, particularly on platforms like Facebook. The strategy showcased the resonance with the target audience, leveraging the strength of community relationships to enhance relatability and engagement. Moving forward, achieving a balance between organic community engagement and corporate resources could enhance the success of future campaigns.

Reasons for Success:
Make Participation Easy: Simplifying the entry process and ensuring clear communication were identified as crucial elements for the success of the campaign.
Inclusive Marketing and Communication: A proactive, accessible, and inclusive strategy targeting communities contributed to success.
Paid Social Media Ads for Visibility: Well-executed paid ads resulted in record-breaking impressions, demonstrating the effectiveness of the campaign’s visibility strategy.
Strategic Planning and Proactive Content Development: Early content development, addressing past feedback, and ensuring audience readiness was critical for success.
Visual Engagement and Social Media Strategy: Organic graphics and a social media takeover, especially on Facebook, played a pivotal role in engaging and reaching the audience.
Impactful Video Content: Video content was a star and including clips on key figures (Doug Ford & SOO Athletes) led to increased engagement and new followers.
